Sandwich

Description:A grilled cheese (sometimes known as a toasted sandwich or cheese toastie) is a hot cheese sandwich typically prepared by heating slices of cheese between slices of bread with a cooking fat such as butter or mayonnaise on a frying pan, griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.

Ingredients:

  • White Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
